How to make a reservation for Trainer Computer Vision usage
Click the plus button

Click the plus button on Trainer Computer Vision Queue List to start making reservation.

Fill the schedule data

Choose a perfect working time for you, then submit. Note that 1 usage session equals to 1 hour.

Wait for approval

While waiting, you please download the computer vision modules and review the materials.

Getting confirmation

When access granted, you'll be given the SBC and Camera Streaming Web credentials by email. Please don't share the credentials to anyone but you. Otherwise, if you're schedule is rejected you'll be notified by email as well.

You are ready!

Proceed the steps by reading the module. Whenever you have trouble, please contact the administrator.
